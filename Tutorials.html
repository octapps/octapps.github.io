1 Tutorials

OctApps provides both general-purpose functions which may be useful for writing Octave programs, and functions specialised to research into continuous gravitational waves. The tutorials in this chapter illustrate several important general-purpose and research-specific functions.

• General-Purpose Functions  Tutorials on some general-purpose OctApps functions.
• Continuous-Wave Functions  Tutorials on some OctApps functions specific to continuous gravitational-wave research.
